Why you should join us!

We all know how hard it can be to get out and explore, so we are giving the opportunities to see what it is like to explore our beautiful country. There are so many places that are amazing to see, this is a way to see them from the comfort of your own home and hopefully come see them for yourselves.

What to look forward to!

VR technology is becoming very advanced and immersive, eventually our website will become compatible. We plan to include walks from all over NZ, and soon branching to different countries. Explore the world and grow with us!
